ℹ️ About TravelMate

TravelMate helps users search for country information and weather, make travels, and plan visit dates.

💡 Features:

  • ✔️ Search for countries with live data
  • ✔️ View current weather conditions
  • ✔️ Add travel notes and visit dates
  • ✔️ Save and manage planned travels (CRUD)
